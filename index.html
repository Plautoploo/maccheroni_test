Master Chef Tips

Secrets from our kitchen

Water is Key

Use 1 liter of water per 100g of pasta. Salt the water only when it starts boiling.

Perfect Timing

Start checking doneness 2 minutes before the suggested cooking time for perfect al dente.

Save the Water

Reserve a cup of pasta water before draining. It's liquid gold for creating silky sauces.
