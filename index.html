Classic Carbonara

Traditional Roman recipe with eggs, pecorino cheese, guanciale, and black pepper.

Classic Carbonara

30 mins 4 servings Medium

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g guanciale
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g pecorino romano
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

While pasta cooks, cut guanciale into small cubes and cook in a pan until crispy.

In a bowl, whisk eggs with grated pecorino and plenty of black pepper.
